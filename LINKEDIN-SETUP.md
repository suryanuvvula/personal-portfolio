# Adding Your Portfolio to LinkedIn üíº

Once deployed, showcase your portfolio on LinkedIn to maximize visibility to recruiters!

## 1. Add to Featured Section

### Steps:

1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
2. Scroll to the **"Featured"** section (or click "Add profile section" if you don't have it)
3. Click **"Add featured"** ‚Üí **"Add link"**
4. Fill in:
   - **URL**: `https://your-portfolio.vercel.app`
   - **Title**: "Professional Portfolio - Full Stack Developer"
   - **Description**: "Interactive portfolio showcasing 10+ years of experience in React, Angular, Node.js, and modern web development. Features projects from Fidelity, Universal Orlando, Best Buy, and more."
5. Click **"Save"**

### Pro Tip:
Take a screenshot of your portfolio's hero section and upload it as the preview image for better visual impact!

---

## 2. Add to Contact Info

1. Click **"Contact info"** on your profile
2. Under **"Websites"**, select **"Portfolio"** from dropdown
3. Enter: `https://your-portfolio.vercel.app`
4. Click **"Save"**
